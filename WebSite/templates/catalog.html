List of Machine- and Human-Intelligent components and services available to be adapted:

Image processing:
  • Optimal Character Recognition (OCR): Extract text from images.
  • Cropping one or several areas (fields).
Text processing:
  • Spell-check: Automatically correct spelling with words from a dictionary.
  • Identification of the language used in a text.
  • Extracting date, country, scientific name, and other fields from a text.
